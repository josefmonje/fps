First-Person Shooter
Made with Angular.js and Three.js


W, A, S, D = Move, MOUSE = Look around, CLICK = Shoot

Hold SHIFT = Run, SPACE = Jump, Q = Reset position

ESC/ALT+TAB = Regain mouse control, Click on screen = Return to first-person control


Please click allow when prompted for permission to disable the mouse control.
You'll probably need to adjust the location of your mouse pointer position and click on the center of the screen.
